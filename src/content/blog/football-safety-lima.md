Peruvian football is known for its intense passion and vibrant atmosphere. However, for many international travelers, navigating the logistics of matchday in Lima can be daunting.

## Why Safety Matters

Lima's stadiums are often located in busy districts where traffic and crowds can become overwhelming. Without local knowledge, getting to and from the stadium safely can be a challenge.

### Key Tips for Travelers

1. **Plan your transport:** Don't rely on finding a taxi right after the game.
2. **Go in groups:** There is safety in numbers.
3. **Know your entrance:** Stadiums have multiple gates, and entering the wrong one can be confusing.
